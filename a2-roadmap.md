# 8-Week Road to A2 — adjusted for Iris

Iris already has broad A1 *vocabulary* (greetings, key verbs, food, countries,
time, family — see progress.md). The real gap to A2 is **grammar, numbers, and
turning known words into correct, connected sentences.** So this plan spends less
time on new words and more on making what she knows work.

~15–20 min/day, 6 days/week, one lighter review day. The progress file overrides
this plan — never rush past shaky material.

## Week 1 — Consolidate + present tense of être/avoir/aller
- Confirm the known vocab is active, not just passive (quick quizzes).
- **Numbers 0–20**, then 0–69.
- Present tense: être (je suis…), avoir (j'ai…), aller (je vais…).
- Build real sentences: "Je suis…", "J'ai…", "Je vais à…".

## Week 2 — Regular -er verbs + articles & gender
- Conjugate -er verbs she knows: parler, aimer, regarder, manger, donner, demander.
- Articles: le/la/les, un/une/des; noun gender patterns.
- Questions: est-ce que, qu'est-ce que, où, quand, combien.
- **Numbers 70–100**, prices ("combien ça coûte ?").

## Week 3 — Futur proche + getting around
- Futur proche: je vais + infinitive (je vais manger, on va aller).
- Faire and other frequent irregulars (prendre, venir).
- Directions and places: à la / au / en, à gauche/droite, tout droit.
- Days of the week, telling time.

## Week 4 — Passé composé (the big one)
- Passé composé with avoir: j'ai mangé, tu as parlé, il a regardé.
- Time markers: hier, la semaine dernière, déjà (reuse "as-tu déjà déjeuné ?").
- Theme: food & restaurant (she already has the vocab — now order fluently).

## Week 5 — Describing + passé composé with être
- Adjective agreement & placement: grand/grande, petit/petite, bon/bonne.
- Passé composé with être (aller, partir → je suis allé(e), je suis parti(e)).
- Comparatives: plus / moins … que.
- Theme: people, family, appearance, weather.

## Week 6 — Likes, plans, opinions
- aimer / préférer / détester + infinitive (extend "j'aime manger…").
- Pronominal verbs: se lever, se coucher, s'appeler.
- Expressing intentions and simple future.
- Theme: hobbies, daily routine, work.

## Week 7 — Connected speech
- Connectors: parce que, mais, donc, alors, et puis.
- Object pronouns (light intro): le/la/les, lui/leur.
- Imperative for requests/instructions.
- Theme: travel, appointments, phone/email basics.

## Week 8 — Consolidation + mock A2
- Intensive review of the weak queue.
- Role-plays: café, directions, introducing yourself, making plans, hotel check-in.
- Short "can-do" self-check against A2 descriptors.
- Celebrate progress; set next goals.

## A2 "can-do" checklist (target by week 8)
- [ ] Introduce myself and others, give basic personal info
- [ ] Use numbers for prices, age, time
- [ ] Conjugate common verbs in present, passé composé, futur proche
- [ ] Order food/drink and shop for simple things
- [ ] Ask for and follow simple directions
- [ ] Talk about my daily routine and past events simply
- [ ] Describe people, places, and the weather
- [ ] Express likes, dislikes, and simple plans
- [ ] Connect ideas with parce que / mais / donc
- [ ] Handle short, predictable everyday exchanges
