Écran 3 — L'examen blanc (style DELF A2)

After every 5 lessons, an exam unlocks. It follows the DELF A2 format (minus the listening part for now — we practise listening together in class): three parts, 25 points each. The app corrects part 1 automatically; for parts 2–3 it shows model answers, and you can copy your writing to me (your tutor) for real correction and a mark.

← Toutes les leçons
Examen 2 · Leçons 6–10
Format DELF A2 · 3 parties · 75 points · ~35 minutes
1Compréhension des écrits/25
Read short texts (a message, a menu, a timetable) and answer multiple-choice questions. Auto-corrected.
« Le train pour Paris part à quinze heures trente. »
À quelle heure part le train ? ○ 15h13  ● 15h30  ○ 5h30
2Production écrite/25
Write two short texts (~60 words) — e.g. describe your day, reply to an invitation. Compare with a model answer, or send it to the tutor for correction.
Copier ma production → correction par le tuteur
3Production orale/25
Speaking cards to do together as a class: introduce yourself, ask questions, role-play (au café, à la gare). Self-scored checklist.
Barème DELF : réussite à partir de 38/75 (50%), minimum 5/25 par partie — same rules as the real exam, so exam day feels familiar. We add listening later when we're ready.
