Écran 3 — L'examen blanc (style DELF A2)

Opened from a gold exam card. Same card layout, three parts like the DELF A2 (listening skipped for now — we practise it together in class). Part 1 is auto-corrected; parts 2–3 show model answers, and your writing can be copied to me for real correction and a mark.

Format DELF A2 · 3 parties · 75 points
1Compréhension des écrits/25
Read short texts (a message, a menu, a timetable) and answer multiple-choice questions. Auto-corrected.
2Production écrite/25
Write two short texts (~60 words). Compare with a model answer, or copy it to the tutor for correction.
3Production orale/25
Speaking cards to do together as a class: se présenter, poser des questions, jeu de rôle (au café, à la gare).
Barème DELF : réussite à partir de 38/75 (50%), minimum 5/25 par partie — same rules as the real exam. Listening can be added later.
